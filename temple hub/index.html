About Jalumuru Hill

Jalumuru Hill is a spiritual haven located in the beautiful landscape of Andhra Pradesh. The hill is home to 24 unique temples, each representing different deities from the Hindu pantheon.

The temples were established through the devotion and contributions of individuals and families from various villages and districts. Each temple has its own story and significance, making Jalumuru Hill a truly special pilgrimage site.

Visitors to Jalumuru Hill can experience peace, tranquility, and spiritual rejuvenation while exploring these sacred temples surrounded by nature's beauty.
